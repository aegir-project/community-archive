1.6 release notes

The Aegir team is very pleased to announce the release of Aegir 1.6!

This release fixes a regression introduced in the 1.5 release that stopped users from being able to compare sites when migrating. It also contains a number of small bug fixes and documentation fixes.

We recommend you upgrade your Aegir system.

For further information on installing Aegir, upgrading to 1.6, or reviewing the changelog, please visit the release notes at http://community.aegirproject.org/1.6

Thanks to our awesome community for their continued support!

The Aegir Team
