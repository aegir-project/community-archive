Hello all,

I'm very pleased to announce that I'll be presenting a couple of sessions at DrupalCon Munich on Aegir:

  • Aegir-based Business Models
  • Fearless development with Drush, Vagrant and Aegir

I'd love to hear suggestions or questions that anyone would like to see addressed in these sessions.

Also, we'll obviously have at least one BoF session, and I'd really like to organize a couple other Aegir-centric get-togethers:

  • Maybe an install party, for those a little newer to the project?
  • A sprint on 2.x, for the more hard-core devs.
  • A contrib showcase, where those building tools for, on or around Aegir, as well as those interested in making use of same in their own deployments.

These could take the form of BoFs, or perhaps just informal meet-ups in and around the Con. Please comment on your interests and plans, as even a modest amount of preparation can make a world of difference.

Cheers, and see you in Munich!
