Office hours

Starting this week we'll be holding 'Office hours' for Aegir in IRC. This will basically be a specific time where one or more of the people more involved in the Aegir project will be around to chat to.

You might want to help us fix some bugs, but aren't sure where to start, or you might want moan about some bug you've found so we can identify the root cause and come up with a fix, pop along and we'll point you in the right direction.

Office hours start this Monday (16th April) and will be held twice weekly from now on.

More details can be found on the Office hours page.
